About

Online video game streaming is a fast growing market with a huge enthusiastic community. This website aims to give an insight in the development of this market by providing a tool to measure popularity and community interests. The provided information hopefully might prove to be of value for game developers and publishers to measure the success of their game, streamers to track and compare their channels development and of course for the community itself.

This site is not an official twitch product. All statistics are based on data gathered via the public twitch API. The information on this site are no indication for the quality and rating of a game or channel.
